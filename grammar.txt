<story>    ::= <sentence> <sentence> <sentence> the end .

<sentence> ::= <subject> <verb> <object> .

<subject>  ::= <article> <optional_adjective> <noun>

<object>   ::= <subject>

<article>  ::= the | a

<noun>     ::= man | cow | car

<verb>     ::= loved | missed | hated | killed

<optional_adjective> ::= big | green | shiny | EPSILON
